Day 1: Exploring Udaipur (14th August 2025)

• Visit the majestic City Palace to experience the grandeur of Mewar rulers
• Relax at Ambrai Ghat/Gangaur Ghat for peaceful riverside views
• Enjoy traditional Rajasthani thali for lunch
• Optional Boat Ride on Lake Pichola (entry ticket not included)
• Shopping at Hathipole Bazaar and Bada Bazaar

Day 2: Udaipur & Journey to Mount Abu (15th August 2025)

• Visit the 17th-century Jagdish Temple, dedicated to Lord Vishnu
• Ropeway ride to Karni Mata Temple for panoramic views
• Visit Bahubali Hills and have a taste of small trek
• Depart for Mount Abu and enjoy an amazing journey through scenic landscapes to reach the only hill station in Rajasthan

Day 3: Mount Abu Adventures & Return (16th - 17th August 2025)

• Experience tranquility at Universal Peace Hall (Brahma Kumaris headquarters)
• Visit Dilwara Temples with intricate marble carvings
• Hike to Guru Shikhar, highest peak in Aravalli Range
• Explore Nakki Lake and enjoy the serene atmosphere
• Visit to local markets for shopping
6:00 PM (16th August): Begin return journey
7:00 AM (17th August): Drop-off in Indore
12:00 PM (17th August): Drop-off in Bhopal

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a Shifoyage trip different?

+
We focus on healing, soulful exploration, and inner connection, not just sightseeing. Our trips are designed to be transformative experiences where you connect with yourself, nature, and a community of like-minded people. You travel with the founder, not just a guide, ensuring a deeply personal and nurturing journey.

What is the group vibe like?

+
Our groups are community-driven, safe, and nurturing. We use fun icebreakers and deep, connection-based activities to turn strangers into friends. You're not a customer; you become part of the Shifoyage family. Many of our travelers form long-lasting bonds that continue long after the trip ends.

I'm a solo traveler. Is this trip for me?

+
Absolutely! A majority of our travelers are solo adventurers. Our trips are the perfect environment to meet new people in a safe and welcoming setting. The group activities and shared experiences make it easy to connect with others.

What kind of accommodation can I expect?

+
We carefully select comfortable and character-filled stays that enhance the journey. We prioritize vibe, safety, and comfort over generic hotels. In Udaipur and Mount Abu, expect cozy rooms with beautiful views and local charm that complement the royal Rajasthani experience.

Is this trip suitable for all age groups?

+
Our trips are designed for young adults and adults who are open to new experiences and connections. Most of our travelers are between 20-35 years old, but we welcome anyone who resonates with our values of fun, connection, and personal growth.
